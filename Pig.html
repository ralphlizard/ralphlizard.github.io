Pigs In Cambodia

3D Models 2013

Pigs in Cambodia (unofficial title) is a collaborative work with Maryyann Landlord and Professor Andrew Ellis Johnson. It is a 3D environment created through Autodesk Maya and Blender in which a couple of pigs are released to wander in a ruined library, a practice used by Pol Pot's regime.

The project was conceived and directed by Professor Johnson, while Maryyann modelled and animated the pigs. All static props were created by me. More updates incoming.
